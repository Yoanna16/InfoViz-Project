About the project

Motivation

Natural disasters can have a life-altering impact on the individuals and families fortunate enough to survive them. But the effect of natural disasters can be felt at the community, city and state level, or many times can impact an entire country. Natural disasters can have huge environmental impacts as well, even when human communities are relatively unaffected. How well the impact of a disaster event is absorbed has much to do with the intensity of the impact and the level of preparedness and resilience of the subject impacted. This project concentrates on the impact of major catastrophes on the patent applications in various parts of the world. The visualisation aims to give an overview of the topic and possible correlations. By doing this we get an insight to either prove or disprove the thesis.

Data

The dataset used in this project was issued by the LMU Munich. Thus we chose to closely follow the values belonging to the geolocations. Additionally we have sourced a number of databases to link information about the natural disasters. For more information on the used datasets please visit our detailed documentation about our sources.
